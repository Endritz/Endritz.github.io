About

Hi, my name's Endrit Zenuni, I'm a graduate student whos interest for technology, programming, and space exploration has led you to this website. My interest in technology started when I was first introduced to a computer and the games that came with them. I've been competing against anybody that is willing to take me on since that point and am currently on the Oakland University Esports team where I compete as a player and help out as a positional coach.
